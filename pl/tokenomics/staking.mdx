Staking is available on **Ethereum** and **BNB Chain**. Lock periods range from no-lock (flexible) to 12 months. Stake at [staking.gamercoin.com](https://staking.gamercoin.com/).

## APRs

### Ethereum

| Lock period | APR | Early unstake |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Flexible (no lock) | 10% | Allowed |
| 3 months | 10% | Not allowed |
| 6 months | 18% | Not allowed |
| 12 months | 25% | Not allowed |

### BNB Chain

| Lock period | APR | Early unstake |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Flexible (no lock) | 6% | Allowed |
| 3 months | 10% | Not allowed |
| 6 months | 18% | Not allowed |
| 12 months | 25% | Not allowed |

<Warning>
Locked stakes cannot be withdrawn before the lock expires. Choose a lock period only if you are comfortable with that liquidity constraint.
</Warning>

<Note>
The advertised maximum APR (25%) assumes the relevant pool is fully subscribed. Effective APR depends on pool capacity and total staked amount at the time of entry.
</Note>
