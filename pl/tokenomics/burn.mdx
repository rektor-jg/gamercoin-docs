A total of **72,000,000 GHX** — **8.1% of the initial supply** — has been permanently removed from circulation across seven burn rounds between **December 2021 and June 2022**.

## Method

Tokens were sent to an unrecoverable burn address (eater address). Once received, they cannot be transferred or withdrawn.

All burn transactions are publicly verifiable on-chain at [gamercoin.com/en/ghx-burning](https://gamercoin.com/en/ghx-burning).
