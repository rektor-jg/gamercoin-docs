Liquidity mining lets you earn GHX on top of standard LP swap fees by staking GHX/BNB LP tokens generated on PancakeSwap.

## Where it runs

| | |
| --- | --- |
| Network | BNB Chain only |
| LP pair | GHX/BNB on PancakeSwap |
| Program dashboard | [staking.gamercoin.com](https://staking.gamercoin.com/) |

<Note>
GHX in ERC-20 form must be bridged to BEP-20 before adding liquidity. Use [cBridge](https://cbridge.celer.network/) or another supported bridge.
</Note>

## How returns work

You earn from two sources:

1. A share of swap fees paid by traders using the GHX/BNB pool (proportional to your LP share).
2. Additional GHX rewards from the GamerHash liquidity-mining program.

Liquidity-mining APRs are typically higher than staking APRs.

<Warning>
Providing liquidity exposes you to **impermanent loss** when the relative prices of GHX and BNB diverge from your deposit ratio. Read [Binance Academy on impermanent loss](https://academy.binance.com/en/articles/impermanent-loss-explained) before participating.
</Warning>
