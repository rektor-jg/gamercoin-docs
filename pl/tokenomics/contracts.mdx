## Bridging

GHX moves between chains via:

- [cBridge](https://cbridge.celer.network/) — Ethereum ↔ BNB Chain
- Bridge from/to Solana via integrations announced in 2025 (Raydium, Jupiter, Solscan). See the [2025 plan](/roadmap/whats-next-2025).

When bridging, supply on the source chain is locked or burned and equivalent supply minted on the destination chain. Total cross-chain supply remains conserved minus burns.
