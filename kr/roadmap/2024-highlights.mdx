The 2024 focus was the GamerHash AI App: first AI image-generation models, early LLM testing, stability work, and the foundation of the GPU earning module.

## Q1

- GamerHash Miner: support for Apple MacBook M2/M3
- AstroGPT upgraded to GPT-4 Turbo
- GHX airdrop for GHXP holders
- Mobile app update

## Q2

- GHXP Boost: higher rewards for holding GHX in MetaMask

- Full redesign of [gamerhash.com](https://gamerhash.com) (desktop + mobile)
- GamerHash AI: Microsoft PHI-4 model added
- DeepSeek (text + coder) models
- Stats navigation improvements; translations for EN, PL, KR, PT
