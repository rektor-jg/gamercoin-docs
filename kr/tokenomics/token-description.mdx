## Utility

**Payments** — In-app currency for the [GamerHash Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/shop) (games, gift cards, vouchers) and access to AI services in the GamerHash AI App.

**Compute rewards** — Distributed to users who share GPU power for AI workloads. The [earning module](https://gamerhash.com/app-download) in the GamerHash AI App is the primary distribution channel.

**Staking** — Lock GHX on Ethereum or BSC for fixed-term APR. See [Staking](/tokenomics/staking).

**Liquidity programs** — Provide GHX/BNB LP tokens on PancakeSwap to earn additional GHX. See [Liquidity Mining](/tokenomics/liquidity-mining).

**Engagement** — Tokens are used in community campaigns, contests, and airdrops, and as access to limited features such as AI Image Booster prompts.
