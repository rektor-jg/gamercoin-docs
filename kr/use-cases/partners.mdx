There are three main integration paths for partners with GamerHash and GHX.

## Play&Earn integrations

Partners create branded tasks inside [Play&Earn](https://gamerhash.com/en/play-and-earn). Users complete the task and receive a GHX or GHXP reward.

| Use case | What partners get |
| --- | --- |
| Lead generation | Targeted reach to gamers and crypto-active users |
| Game launches | Onboarding flow for new releases (downloads, account creation, tutorials) |
| Token launches | Awareness via wallet setup, swap, or staking tasks |
| Engagement campaigns | Retention loops via repeatable tasks |

Tracking is integrated server-to-server. Settlement happens on completion of the partner's defined attribution window.

## GamerHash Store

The [GamerHash Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/shop) sells games, gift cards, vouchers, and digital goods to GamerHash users. Partners can list inventory and sponsor banner placements across GamerHash marketing channels.

## Referral program

Affiliates (creators, streamers, communities) earn passive GHX for users they bring to the platform. Rewards continue as referees mine, earn, or spend on the platform.

The program is open to any GamerHash account. Details and tracking link generation are at [gamerhash.com/en/account/referral-program](https://gamerhash.com/en/account/referral-program).
