There are two main ways to **get** **GamerCoin (GHX):**

  1. **On the market** – through centralized (CEX) and decentralized (DEX) exchanges.

  2. **Within the GamerHash ecosystem** – by sharing GPU power via the **GamerHash AI App** and receiving GHX as rewards.


## Market

GHX is available on both centralized (CEX) and decentralized (DEX) exchanges, giving traders multiple options to buy and trade the token.

#### **Market**

The first listing of GHX took place on **April 8, 2021** , right in the middle of the bull run that brought global attention to utility and gaming tokens. Entering the market at that time positioned GHX among the new wave of digital assets driving innovation in Web3 and marked the beginning of its trading journey on both centralized (CEX) and decentralized (DEX) exchanges.

#### **Bithumb** GHX had its first exchange listing on **Bithumb** , making the token available to South Korean users with the trading pair [GHX/KRW](<https://www.bithumb.com/react/trade/order/GHX-KRW>). Bithumb is one of the largest exchanges in Korea — a market known for its strong activity in both crypto and gaming.

**KuCoin**

GHX is listed on **KuCoin** , where it can be traded against USDT ([GHX/USDT](<https://www.kucoin.com/trade/GHX-USDT>)). KuCoin is a globally recognized exchange, giving GHX exposure to a wide international user base. 

#### MEXC

In early 2024, GHX was added to **MEXC** , providing additional liquidity and the [GHX/USDT](<https://www.mexc.com/exchange/GHX_USDT>) trading pair for international users. MEXC’s strong retail community further expanded the accessibility of GHX.

## The GamerHash platform

Within the **GamerHash platform** , there are two main ways to get GHX:

  * **Sharing computing power** – users who contribute their GPU resources to the GamerHash network are rewarded with GHX.

  * **Converting GHXP points** – accumulated **GamerHash Experience Points (GHXP)** can be exchanged into GHX, creating an additional earning path for active participants.


GHX earned on the platform is credited to user wallets and can be withdrawn to an external wallet, or spent directly in the **GamerHash Store** on games and gift cards.

### Other activities

In addition, GHX is distributed through **community initiatives** such as airdrops, contests, and special campaigns shared on our social channels. Holders can also earn GHX through dedicated **staking** and **liquidity mining programs**.
