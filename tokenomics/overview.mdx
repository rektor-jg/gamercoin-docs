**GamerCoin (GHX)** is a cryptocurrency that serves as the official digital currency of the GamerHash platform. It is used for transactions and payments within the GamerHash Store, as well as for rewarding users who share their processing power for cryptocurrency mining and providing **GPU power** for **GamerHash AI** and **blockchain operations**. It’s used as a means of payment at GamerHash AI both from users and partners needing GPU power perspective.

GHX can be used to **purchase** **games** , **software** , **hardware** , **gift cards** , and other products available on the GamerHash platform. It can also be earned through the Play&Earn feature, which allows gamers to earn virtual currency by playing games. GHX has several practical applications, as it provides a fast, secure, and cost-effective way to make purchases and access a variety of digital products.

GamerCoin holders will also have the possibility to use the **AI products** such as Image Generation on the GamerHash AI platform.

GHX is a **versatile cryptocurrency** that has a variety of uses within the GamerHash platform. Its widespread availability and usability make it a valuable tool for gamers who are looking for a more accessible and cost-effective way to purchase games and other digital products. Additionally, it offers a unique way for gamers to earn rewards and engage with **emerging blockchain** and **AI technologies**.
