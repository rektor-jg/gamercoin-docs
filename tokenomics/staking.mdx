Staking lets you lock GHX for a fixed period and earn APR. It's the simplest way to put an existing position to work — no LP setup, no impermanent loss, no active management. Just deposit, hold, and claim.

The dashboard is at [staking.gamercoin.com](https://staking.gamercoin.com/).

## Where you can stake

| Chain | Available since | Why pick this chain |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ethereum | Original launch | Largest GHX float; integrate easily with most DeFi tooling |
| BNB Chain | Original launch | Lowest fees on EVM; easiest to enter for first-time stakers |
| Solana | 2025 Q2 | Fast finality, lowest cost; same APR tiers |

## APR tiers

The headline rate is **25% APR** on the 12-month lock. Shorter locks earn proportionally less; flexible (no-lock) is lowest because liquidity stays with you.

| Lock period | Ethereum | BNB Chain | Solana | Early unstake |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Flexible (no lock) | 10% | 6% | 6% | Allowed |
| 3 months | 10% | 10% | 10% | Not allowed |
| 6 months | 18% | 18% | 18% | Not allowed |
| 12 months | 25% | 25% | 25% | Not allowed |

<Warning>
Locked stakes cannot be withdrawn before the lock expires. Pick a lock period only if you are comfortable with that liquidity constraint for the full duration.
</Warning>

<Note>
The advertised maximum APR (25%) assumes the relevant pool is fully subscribed. Effective APR depends on pool capacity and total staked at the time of entry. Always check current rates on the dashboard before committing.
</Note>

## Picking a lock period

| If you... | Pick |
| --- | --- |
| Need the position liquid for trading or other use | **Flexible** — give up some yield for the option to exit |
| Want predictable yield over a quarter | **3 months** — modest commitment, modest premium |
| Are confident in a 6-month horizon | **6 months** — yield jumps to 18% |
| Are happy to lock for a year for the highest rate | **12 months** — full 25% headline APR |
