## What GHX does

**Payments** — Spend GHX in the [GamerHash Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/shop) for games, gift cards, and vouchers, or inside the GamerHash AI App to unlock the AI Image Booster prompts.

**Compute rewards** — Share GPU power for AI workloads through the [GamerHash AI App](https://gamerhash.com/pl/app-download) earning module. Earnings are paid in **GUSD** (the in-app USD unit) and convertible to GHX in one click — so the user takes on the GHX exposure only when they want to.

**Staking** — Lock GHX on Ethereum, BNB Chain, or Solana for a fixed term and earn APR — up to **25%** at the longest lock. See [Staking](/tokenomics/staking).

**Liquidity programs** — Provide LP on Uniswap (Ethereum), PancakeSwap (BNB Chain), or Raydium (Solana) to earn swap fees plus additional GHX rewards on top. See [Liquidity Mining](/tokenomics/liquidity-mining).

**Holder tier** — Holding and using GHX in the AI App lifts your in-app tier, which raises your GHXP multiplier. A higher multiplier means more GHX paid out per action — the token compounds the more you hold and use it.
