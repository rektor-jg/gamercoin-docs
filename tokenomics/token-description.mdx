The ever-growing economy of GamerHash is fueled by its native digital asset — **GamerCoin (GHX)**. Originally launched on **Ethereum** , GHX was later bridged to **BNB Chain** and **Solana** , making it widely accessible across different blockchain ecosystems. Our whitepaper was also registered in Malta, underscoring GamerHash’s early commitment to transparency and compliance.

**GHX is the foundation of all transactions within the ecosystem** and has multiple uses: 

🛍 **Payments** – used to make purchases in the **GamerHash Store**. 🎮 **Engagement** – grants access to dedicated competitions, events, and community incentives. 💎 **Rewards** – distributed to participants for sharing GPU power through the **GamerHash AI App**. 

GHX continues to evolve as the GamerHash ecosystem grows — connecting compute power, AI, and blockchain into one economy.
