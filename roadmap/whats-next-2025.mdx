### Overview

In 2025, GamerHash is focused on expanding both **AI capabilities** and the **utility of GHX**. Upcoming milestones include:

  * **Advanced AI features** — new tools such as image-to-video generation, custom model deployment, and voice-based applications, all running privately on users’ GPUs.

  * **Stronger token utility** — extending GHX use cases with bridging to more ecosystems, staking mechanisms, and new ways to reward long-term holders.

  * **Broader accessibility** — scaling our **deAPI** initiative to make distributed compute available to developers, startups, and enterprises through a simple API.

  * **Community growth** — new engagement programs designed to strengthen the GamerHash community and bring more users into the AI + compute economy.


2025 marks a decisive step toward building a **multi-modal, decentralized compute ecosystem** that connects gamers, AI projects, and Web3 — all powered by **GamerCoin (GHX)**.
