GamerHash began humbly in November 2017, when two friends set out to democratize cryptocurrency mining by enabling anyone to earn using idle computing power. Our first product was the **GamerHash Miner App** , designed to make crypto mining as simple as running software in the background. In just a few months, the idea resonated — 150,000 users had joined by March 2018, and the community quickly grew to ~300,000 by August.

From the very beginning, our goal was bigger than mining. We envisioned a **distributed supercomputer built from thousands of home PCs**. Mining was simply the low-hanging fruit that allowed users to start earning instantly. The launch of our native token, **GamerCoin (GHX)** , in 2019–2020 created the foundation for a growing ecosystem.

Over time, the focus has shifted. With the rise of open-source AI models, we saw a new opportunity: instead of only mining crypto, users could **run AI workloads locally on their own GPUs**. This marked the transition to the **GamerHash AI App** — giving people not just passive income, but also access to AI tools like image and video generation, fully private and available 24/7.

Today, with nearly 800,000 registered users, GamerHash is evolving into a global platform that blends GPU sharing and AI services — all driven by the vision of turning home PCs into a decentralized supercomputer **Powering AI**.
