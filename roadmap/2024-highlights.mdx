**Overview**

In 2024, we focused on developing the **GamerHash AI App** — testing the first AI models for image generation and experimenting with early LLMs, while improving stability and preparing the platform for scale. Key milestones included:

  * **User experience upgrades** — simplifying onboarding, streamlining the app, and making it easier than ever to start earning.

  * **AI functionality expansion** — adding features that enabled users to experiment with AI tools and running early tests of the earning module powered by GPUs.

  * **Fresh branding** — a modernized identity that reflects our shift from mining towards AI and decentralized compute.

  * **Global reach** — adding multilingual support and opening GamerHash to an even wider audience.


These improvements set the stage for what’s next: advanced AI features, deeper GHX utility, and broader ecosystem integrations coming in 2025.
