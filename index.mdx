GHX is the native utility token of [GamerHash](https://gamerhash.com) — the network that turns idle consumer GPUs into compute for AI workloads. The token isn't an abstract claim on a future product. It is the settlement layer for a distributed compute network already serving real users, real partners, and real AI applications.

## How the GHX economy works

```mermaid
graph LR
    U([App users]):::user
    D{{DePIN GPU network}}:::depin
    G((GHX)):::token
    P([Partners & developers]):::partner

    U -->|Share idle GPU| D
    D -->|Compute supply| P
    P -->|Pay for compute| G
    G -->|Buyback & burn<br/>Rewards & APR| U

    classDef user fill:#2AD46F,stroke:#1FB85C,color:#0F1115
    classDef depin fill:#E83C41,stroke:#C53034,color:#fff
    classDef token fill:#F59E0B,stroke:#D97706,color:#0F1115
    classDef partner fill:#9945FF,stroke:#7e2bdc,color:#fff
```

Users contribute GPU power. The DePIN aggregates and routes it to AI workloads. Partners and developers pay for compute. A portion of revenue is used to buy GHX from the open market and burn it; the rest is distributed to contributors. The loop self-reinforces as more partners join.

## Partners and integrations

GHX and the GamerHash GPU network are integrated with infrastructure providers, AI builders, and Web3 protocols including:

[Aethir](https://aethir.com) · [BNB Chain](https://www.bnbchain.org) · [Celer Network](https://celer.network) · [Chainlink](https://chain.link) · [Exabits](https://exabits.ai) · [IQNet](https://iqnet.io) · [PAAL AI](https://paalai.ai) · [The Sandbox](https://www.sandbox.game)

Real products running on the network include **HUMAIN** (live consumer product) and **GenTok** (AI-powered content for Web3 communities). The [deAPI platform](https://deapi.ai) brings the same distributed compute to any developer through an OpenAI-style API and is now an official n8n node.
