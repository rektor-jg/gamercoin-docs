The pages in this section are reproduced from the official GHX whitepaper, registered with the **Malta Financial Services Authority (MFSA)**. They cover the issuing entity, AML/CFT policy, and a disclaimer of liability.

<Note>
**TL;DR.** This documentation and the whitepaper are **informational only** — they are not legal, financial, tax, or investment advice. They are also not legally binding: ownership and rights to GHX are governed exclusively by the terms of each transaction. If you are unsure whether transacting in GHX is permitted in your jurisdiction, consult a qualified professional.
</Note>
