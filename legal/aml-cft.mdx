## Regulatory status

Under the Prevention of Money Laundering and Funding of Terrorism Regulations (**PMLFTR**), the Company does not qualify as a "subject person" — it does not carry out any "relevant financial business" or "relevant activity." It is therefore not subject to the responsibilities and obligations imposed by AML/CFT legislation in this jurisdiction.

## Voluntary controls

Notwithstanding the above, the Company implements the following risk-mitigation measures:

- Due-diligence documentation and screening on investors participating in private sales.
- A policy of declining to deal with third parties that present, on the available evidence, a high risk of money laundering or terrorism financing.
